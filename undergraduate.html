Undergraduate Study

Academics / Undergraduate study

The University maintains students' admission into the bachelor's degree programmes according to the approved guidelines and processes. For the bachelor's degree, applicants are admitted through the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME), Pre-Degree Science (PDS) programme, Direct Entry (DE) and Inter-University Transfer (IUT).

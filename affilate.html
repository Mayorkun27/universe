Universe University Affiliates

Partnering for Excellence

At Universe University, we believe in the power of collaboration to advance education, research, and community impact. Our affiliate partnerships enable us to work closely with leading institutions, organizations, and industry partners to create opportunities that benefit our students, faculty, and the broader community driving us all to mutual growth and success. Here is a list of our current affilations:
  • Osun State College of Education, Ila-Orangun
  • PAN African College of Education, Offa and Lagos State
  • Federal College of Dental and Theraphy College, Enugu
  • Institute of Social Work, Nigeria (ISOWN), Lagos
  • Standard Trust Educational Consultancy, (STECO) University of Ibadan
  • Living Spring College of Innovation and Technology (LISCOTECH), Osogbo
  • Ajayi Polytechnic, Ikere Ekiti
  • The College of Education, Ilorin
  • Institute of Governance and Leadership Studies in Africa (IGLSA) Abuja
  • Institute of Governance and Leadership Studies in Africa/Entra Young Limited, Lagos
  • MONA Institute of Hospitality and Tourism, Abuja
  • Citi Polytechnic, Abuja
  • African Institute of Health Professionals, (AIHP), Abuja
  • Mayday College, Ibadan/Awe
  • Heritage College of Management and Technology, Okinni, Osun State
  • Oyo State College of Advanced Nursing and Midwifery, Ibadan
  • Federal Cooperative College, Ibadan
  • Diplomats Institute of Business Studies (DIBBS), Uyo, Akwa-Ibom
  • Questville Education Center, Osogbo
  • Squap Olatunde/Igbajo Polytechnic CAMPU
  • Winners Model College, Osogbo
